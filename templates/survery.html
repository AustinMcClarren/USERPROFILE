Data Collection Survey

Demographic Questions

1. What is your age range?

2. What is your gender?

3. What is your household size?

4. How many rooms are there in your house?

5. How many people live in each room?

6. What is your educational level?

7. What is your annual household income?

8. What is your ethnic or racial background?

9. What is your primary language spoken at home?

10. Do you or any member of your household have any disabilities?

11. What is your employment status?

12. Do you currently receive any government assistance programs?

Housing-related Questions

1. How long have you lived in your current home?

2. What type of housing do you live in?

3. Are you the homeowner or a renter?

4. Is your home in need of repairs or renovations?

5. Are you interested in improving the energy efficiency of your home?

6. Are you aware of any existing renewable energy programs or initiatives in your community?

7. What are your primary concerns or barriers when it comes to accessing clean energy technologies or home remodeling?

8. How would you rate the overall condition of your home?

9. What is the approximate square footage of your home?

10. How many bedrooms does your home have?

11. Do you own any energy-efficient appliances in your home?

12. Are you interested in incorporating renewable energy sources (such as solar panels) into your home?

13. Have you ever received any financial assistance or grants for home improvements?

14. How would you describe your neighborhood's access to public transportation?

Home Remodeling and Renovations

1. Have you previously undertaken any home remodeling or renovation projects?

2. If yes, what types of remodeling or renovation projects have you completed? (Select all that apply)

3. Are you currently planning or considering any home remodeling or renovation projects?

4. What factors would influence your decision to pursue a home remodeling or renovation project? (Select all that apply)

5. Are you aware of any local programs or incentives that support home remodeling or renovation projects?

Renewable Energy and Home Energy Efficiency

1. Are you interested in installing renewable energy systems in your home? (e.g., solar panels)

2. What factors would motivate you to adopt renewable energy technologies? (Select all that apply)

3. Are you familiar with different renewable energy options available for residential use? (e.g., solar, wind, geothermal)

4. What concerns or barriers do you have regarding the adoption of renewable energy technologies? (Select all that apply)

5. Are you interested in receiving a home energy audit or assessment to identify areas for energy efficiency improvements?

6. What specific energy efficiency upgrades or measures are you most interested in? (Select all that apply)

7. Would you be interested in attending workshops or educational sessions on energy-saving practices and technologies?

Financial Assistance and Financing Options

1. Would you be interested in learning about financial assistance programs or grants available for home improvements and clean energy initiatives?

2. Have you encountered challenges in obtaining financing for home remodeling or energy-related projects?

3. Would you benefit from guidance or support in navigating the process of applying for loans or financial assistance programs?
